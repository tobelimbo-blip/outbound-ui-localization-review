# E-commerce and Membership Glossary

Separate retail actions, fulfillment states, loyalty value, and eligibility. Do not promise savings or benefits that the source does not guarantee.

| Chinese / Intent | Recommended | Avoid | Context | Notes | Risk |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 购物车 | Cart / Shopping cart | Shopping bag | E-commerce | Follow regional and product conventions. | Low |
| 结账 | Checkout | Settle accounts | Cart, purchase | Use `Checkout` as a noun or CTA according to style. | High |
| 订单 | Order | Booking | Retail order | Keep separate from subscriptions and travel bookings. | Medium |
| 已下单 | Order placed | Ordered successfully | Order status | Do not imply payment or fulfillment. | Medium |
| 配送 | Delivery / Shipping | Distribution | Fulfillment | `Shipping` is dispatch; `delivery` is arrival to the customer. | High |
| 已发货 | Shipped / Dispatched | Delivered | Order status | Do not imply receipt. | High |
| 已送达 | Delivered | Arrived | Order status | Confirm the carrier event represented. | High |
| 优惠码 | Promo code / Promotion code | Discount password | Checkout | Both forms are acceptable; use the shorter form in compact UI. | Low |
| 优惠券 | Coupon / Voucher | Discount ticket | Wallet, checkout | Confirm regional usage and redemption rules. | Medium |
| 退货 | Return | Refund | After-sales | A return and refund are separate processes. | High |
| 退款 | Refund | Return money | After-sales | State status, amount, and destination when material. | High |
| 积分 | Points / Reward points | Scores | Loyalty | Use the program's official currency name when available. | Medium |
| 获取积分 | Earn points | Receive points always | Loyalty | Do not imply eligibility before conditions are met. | Medium |
| 兑换积分 | Redeem points | Exchange points | Rewards | Distinguish redeeming points from converting them. | Medium |
| 会员等级 | Membership tier | Member level | Loyalty | Use approved tier names and qualification rules. | Medium |
| 会员权益 | Membership benefits | Member rights | Loyalty | Do not invent eligibility or availability. | High |
| 推荐奖励 | Referral reward | Recommendation prize | Growth | Clarify inviter, invitee, trigger, and timing. | High |
| 邀请奖励 | Invite bonus | Invite gift guaranteed | Growth | Avoid guaranteed language if conditions apply. | High |
| 立即领取 | Claim now | Get now | Reward CTA | Confirm the user is eligible to claim. | High |
| 缺货 | Out of stock | Sold out forever | Product status | `Sold out` may imply no restock; use product convention. | Medium |
| 补货通知 | Notify me / Back-in-stock alert | Restock reminder success | Product CTA | Do not imply subscription succeeded before confirmation. | Medium |
