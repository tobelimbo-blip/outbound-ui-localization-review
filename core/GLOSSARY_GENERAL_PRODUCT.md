# General Product Glossary

Use this glossary as context, not as a one-to-one translation dictionary. Prefer the product's approved terminology and adjust for component, sentence role, and user state.

| Chinese / Intent | Recommended | Avoid | Context | Notes | Risk |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 账户 | Account | User center | Navigation, settings | Use `account` for the user's product identity or account area. | Medium |
| 个人资料 | Profile | Personal data | Account UI | Use `personal information` only when referring to regulated data. | Medium |
| 设置 | Settings | Setup | Navigation, page title | `Setup` usually describes an onboarding process. | Low |
| 通知 | Notifications | Notices | Product inbox, preferences | Use `notice` for a specific formal announcement. | Low |
| 权限 | Permission / Access | Authority | Settings, enterprise tools | Use `permission` for an allowed action and `access` for resource availability. | High |
| 隐私 | Privacy | Secrecy | Settings, policy | Do not invent privacy promises or policy scope. | High |
| 安全 | Security | Safety | Account, authentication | `Safety` is usually physical or personal safety. | High |
| 验证 | Verification | Validation | Identity, email, payment | Use `validation` for checking data format or correctness. | High |
| 订阅 | Subscription | Subscribe plan | Billing, content, SaaS | Distinguish subscription status from the action `Subscribe`. | High |
| 账单 | Billing / Bill / Invoice | Billing list | Account, finance | `Billing` is the area or process; `bill` or `invoice` is a document or amount. | High |
| 套餐 | Plan | Package | Subscription, pricing | `Package` may fit travel bundles, not most SaaS pricing. | Medium |
| 升级 | Upgrade | Promote | Plan, feature | Confirm price and effective time when material. | Medium |
| 降级 | Downgrade | Reduce level | Plan, feature | State lost features or timing when consequential. | High |
| 试用 | Trial / Free trial | Try use | Pricing, onboarding | Do not imply `free` unless the offer is free. | Medium |
| 邀请 | Invite | Invitation to | Button, collaboration | Buttons usually use `Invite`; nouns may use `Invitation`. | Low |
| 分享 | Share | Forward | Button, content | Use `Forward` only for messages or email. | Low |
| 保存 | Save | Preserve | Button, form | Use `Save changes` when the object is otherwise unclear. | Low |
| 提交 | Submit | Commit | Form, application | Name the object for high-impact submissions. | Medium |
| 确认 | Confirm | Ensure | Button, modal | A standalone `Confirm` is acceptable only when the decision is clear. | Medium |
| 取消 | Cancel | Abandon | Button, flow | Distinguish dismissing a dialog from cancelling an order or service. | High |
| 移除 | Remove | Delete | List, membership | `Remove` may be reversible or scoped; do not replace it with `Delete`. | Medium |
| 删除 | Delete | Remove | Destructive action | State the object and consequence when irreversible. | High |
| 编辑 | Edit | Modify | Button, menu | `Edit` is the common compact UI action. | Low |
| 查看详情 | View details | Check details | Link, button | `Details` can work when the surrounding pattern is clear. | Low |
| 了解更多 | Learn more | Know more | Link, marketing | Keep lowercase `more` in sentence case. | Low |
| 开始使用 | Get started | Start use | Onboarding, CTA | Use a more specific action when the next step is known. | Low |
| 继续 | Continue | Go on | Flow navigation | Do not use if the action submits or charges. | Medium |
| 完成 | Done / Finish | Complete it | Button, flow | `Done` closes or confirms; `Finish` completes a process. | Medium |
| 返回 | Back | Return | Navigation | `Return` may imply returning an item or going to a named destination. | Low |
| 关闭 | Close | Shut down | Dialog, panel | Use `Turn off` or `Disable` for a feature state. | Medium |
| 启用 / 停用 | Enable / Disable | Open / Close | Settings, permissions | Confirm whether the change is immediate or reversible. | Medium |
