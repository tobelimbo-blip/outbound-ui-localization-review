# Operation and Marketing Glossary

Keep campaign wording attractive without adding unsupported urgency, exclusivity, savings, eligibility, or guarantees.

| Chinese / Intent | Recommended | Avoid | Context | Notes | Risk |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 活动 | Campaign / Event | Activity | Marketing, operations | Use `event` for an actual event and `campaign` for a promotion. | Low |
| 优惠 | Offer / Savings | Preferential | Promotion | Do not imply a discount if the benefit is not price-based. | Medium |
| 奖励 | Reward | Prize | Loyalty, referral | `Prize` usually comes from a contest or draw. | Medium |
| 代金券 | Voucher | Cash coupon | Checkout, wallet | Confirm whether it has stored value or usage restrictions. | High |
| 优惠券 | Coupon | Voucher | E-commerce | Regional usage varies; follow the product's existing term. | Medium |
| 优惠码 | Promotion code / Promo code | Discount code | Field label, checkout | `Promo code` is compact; `Promotion code` is more formal. | Low |
| 推荐有礼 | Referral reward | Recommend gift | Referral campaign | State who receives the reward and when. | High |
| 邀请奖励 | Invite bonus / Referral bonus | Invitation award | Membership, growth | Confirm eligibility and reward conditions. | High |
| 限时优惠 | Limited-time offer | Time-limited preference | Campaign | Include an end time when it affects eligibility. | High |
| 专享权益 | Exclusive benefit | Exclusive right | Membership | Do not claim exclusivity without a defined audience. | Medium |
| 会员权益 | Membership benefits | Member rights | Loyalty, subscription | `Rights` may sound legal. | Medium |
| 欢迎礼 | Welcome gift | Welcome courtesy | Onboarding, membership | Confirm whether it is guaranteed or conditional. | Medium |
| 新人优惠 | New user offer | Newcomer discount | Acquisition | Confirm the definition of `new user`. | High |
| 精选 | Featured / Curated | Selected excellent | Content, products | `Curated` implies editorial selection. | Low |
| 推荐 | Recommended | Recommend | Label, section | Avoid implying personalization unless it is personalized. | Medium |
| 热门 | Popular | Hot | Content, products | `Hot` can work in informal retail contexts only. | Low |
| 趋势 | Trending | Trendy | Content, search | Use only when based on current activity. | Medium |
| 条款适用 | Terms apply | Rules apply | Promotion | Link to approved terms; do not generate them from scratch. | High |
| 了解更多 | Learn more | Know more | Link | Use when the destination provides meaningful detail. | Low |
| 立即领取 | Claim now | Receive now | Reward, voucher | `Claim` may imply eligibility; confirm availability. | High |
| 兑换 | Redeem | Exchange | Rewards, vouchers | Use `exchange` for currencies or item swaps, not loyalty redemption. | Medium |
| 赚取积分 | Earn points | Get points | Loyalty | Avoid implying every action qualifies. | Medium |
| 使用积分 | Use points / Redeem points | Spend scores | Checkout, rewards | Use `Redeem points` when obtaining a reward. | Medium |
| 闪购 | Flash sale | Quick sale | E-commerce | Use only for a genuinely time-limited sale. | High |
