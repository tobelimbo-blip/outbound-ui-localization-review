# SaaS and Enterprise Glossary

Distinguish organizations, workspaces, roles, permissions, plans, seats, and billing states. Follow the product's actual access model.

| Chinese / Intent | Recommended | Avoid | Context | Notes | Risk |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 工作区 | Workspace | Workbench | SaaS navigation | `Workbench` may fit a specialized operational product, not generic SaaS. | Medium |
| 组织 | Organization | Company account | Enterprise admin | Confirm whether one company can own multiple organizations. | Medium |
| 团队 | Team | Group | Collaboration | Follow the product's hierarchy. | Low |
| 成员 | Member | User | Team, organization | A user may belong to multiple teams or organizations. | Medium |
| 所有者 | Owner | Administrator | Roles | Ownership may include billing or destructive permissions. | High |
| 管理员 | Admin / Administrator | Manager | Roles | Use `Admin` in compact UI and `Administrator` in formal copy. | High |
| 角色 | Role | Identity | Access control | A role groups permissions; it is not the user's identity. | High |
| 权限 | Permission | Authority | Access control | Name the affected action or resource. | High |
| 访问权限 | Access | Visit permission | Resource access | Clarify view, edit, share, or admin access. | High |
| 邀请成员 | Invite members | Add users directly | Team management | Do not imply membership before acceptance when an invite is sent. | Medium |
| 席位 | Seat | Position | Pricing, licensing | Clarify assigned, available, or billable seats. | High |
| 套餐 | Plan | Package | Pricing | Use approved plan names. | Medium |
| 升级套餐 | Upgrade plan | Promote plan | Billing | State price and effective timing when consequential. | High |
| 降级套餐 | Downgrade plan | Lower package | Billing | State feature loss and effective timing. | High |
| 免费试用 | Free trial | Trial free forever | Onboarding | Confirm duration, card requirement, and renewal behavior. | High |
| 账单 | Billing | Billings | Admin navigation | `Billing` is the common section label. | High |
| 自动续订 | Auto-renew | Automatically repurchase | Subscription | Use approved cancellation and renewal terms. | High |
| 审计日志 | Audit log | Operation record | Enterprise admin | Do not imply completeness beyond recorded events. | High |
| 单点登录 | Single sign-on / SSO | One-click login | Authentication | Do not confuse SSO with social login. | High |
| 同步 | Sync | Synchronize done | Data, integration | State direction and last sync status when relevant. | Medium |
| 导出 | Export | Download | Data, report | `Download` may be the result; `Export` describes data generation. | Medium |
| 发布 | Publish | Submit | Content, configuration | Clarify visibility and whether the action is reversible. | High |
